Privacy Policy

Ensuring your culinary journey is as secure as it is delicious. Learn how we handle your data with prestige and care.

Data Collection

We collect personal information you voluntarily provide when purchasing tickets, registering for events, or subscribing to our newsletter. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and payment information. We also collect anonymous usage data to improve your experience.

Ticket Purchase Security

All ticket transactions are encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols. Your payment information is processed securely through our PCI-compliant payment partners and is never stored on our servers. We implement multi-factor authentication for account access.

Cookie Usage

We use essential cookies to ensure the proper functioning of our website. Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our site, allowing us to enhance your experience. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may opt out of marketing communications by unsubscribing or contacting our support team. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. For data requests, please contact our privacy team.

Data Sharing

We may share your information with trusted partners who assist in event operations, such as payment processors and venue security. These partners are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. Ticket purchase records are retained for financial and legal compliance purposes.
